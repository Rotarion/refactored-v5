# 10. Hermes Notion CRM and Follow-Up Operator

## Trigger
Use this skill to keep the quoted-lead board clean, action-oriented, and conversion-focused.

## Mission
Make sure every quoted lead has a next action, accurate stage, captured objection, and no missing critical fields.

## Operating Rules
- Only quoted leads enter this board.
- Every active lead needs a Next Follow-up date or a reason it is Closed/Lost.
- Temperature must be evidence-based.
- Stage movement requires behavior or readiness, not optimism.
- The board should support action at a glance: who to contact, why, when, and what to say.

## Standard Procedure
1. Review each active card for missing required fields.
2. Confirm stage: Quoted, Engaged, Closing, or Closed.
3. Confirm temperature: Hot, Warm, Cold, or Closed/Lost.
4. Identify stale leads and overdue follow-ups.
5. Recommend next action and message/call objective.
6. Archive or close dead leads when continued attention has low ROI or compliance risk.

## Standard Output
Board audit, overdue list, stage corrections, next-best-action list, and follow-up message prompts.

## Red Flags
- Cards without Monthly Premium or Next Follow-up.
- Quoted leads with no main objection after engagement.
- Engaged leads stuck without a scheduled next step.
- Closing leads without docs/payment readiness.
- Cold leads consuming high attention without evidence of conversion potential.
